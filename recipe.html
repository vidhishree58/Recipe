Creamy Garlic Parmesan Pasta

Ingredients:

  1. 200 g spaghetti (or any pasta of choice)
  2. 2 tbsp olive oil
  3. 3 cloves garlic (finely chopped)
  4. 1 tbsp butter
  5. 1 cup fresh cream
  6. ½ cup grated parmesan cheese
  7. ½ tsp chili flakes (optional)
  8. ½ tsp black pepper
  9. Salt (to taste)
  10. Fresh parsley (for garnish)

Instructions:

  • Boil pasta in salted water until al dente.
  • Drain the pasta and save ½ cup of the pasta water.
  • In a pan, heat olive oil and a little butter.
  • Add chopped garlic and sauté until fragrant (do not burn).
  • Pour in fresh cream and stir well.
  • Mix in grated parmesan cheese, chili flakes, black pepper, and salt.
  • Add the boiled pasta and toss gently to coat with the sauce.
  • If the sauce is too thick, add some of the reserved pasta water.
  • Garnish with freshly chopped parsley and extra parmesan.
  • Serve hot with garlic bread.

Peri-Peri Fries

Ingredients:

  1. 3–4 medium potatoes (or 250 g frozen fries)
  2. 2 tbsp peri-peri spice mix
  3. 1 tbsp cornflour (optional, for extra crispiness)
  4. Salt (to taste)
  5. Oil (for frying)
  6. 1 tsp lemon juice (optional, for tangy flavor)
  7. Optional: additional seasoning like paprika or black pepper
  8. Optional: chopped fresh herbs for garnish (e.g., parsley or coriander)

Instructions:

  • Wash, peel, and cut potatoes into thin strips.
  • Soak in cold water for 20–30minutes.
  • Drain and pat dry completely using a clean towel.
  • Toss the potato strips with cornflour and a pinch of salt.
  • Heat oil & fry the potatoes on medium flame until soft (1st fry).
  • Remove, let them cool.
  • Then fry again on high flame until golden and crispy (2nd fry).
  • Immediately sprinkle peri-peri spice mix over the hot fries.
  • Add a few drops of lemon juice if you like extra tangy flavor.
  • Serve hot with mayonnaise, ketchup, or cheesy dip.
